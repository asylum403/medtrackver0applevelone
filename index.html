📸 How to take a clear photo
  • Use the paper sheet if you have it. Photograph the printed information sheet from your pharmacy — flat paper comes out clearer than a rounded bottle.
  • Get close and hold steady so the label fills the screen and the words look sharp before you tap.
  • Use good light, avoid glare. Turn the camera flash off if it washes out the words.
  • Keep it flat and straight on — flatten the paper, or turn the bottle so the label faces you.
  • One medication or supplement per photo.

Here are the two things you can do. Pick one — you can always come back here for the other.

⏰ Turn on your reminders

This adds a reminder to your phone for every dose and every refill — it beeps at each time, even when this page is closed. Each medication's reminders run until its supply runs out (based on the count you entered) — with a refill reminder before then — so the alarms stop on their own when you're out.

💊 Picked up a refill? Paste your saved list back in first (under "Returning? Restore a saved list" on the Add screen) and update the count. The new reminders pick up where your current ones end — so you won't get two alarms at the same time for the same medicine.

    Tip: if a relative or pharmacist is nearby, this takes them about 30 seconds to help with.
