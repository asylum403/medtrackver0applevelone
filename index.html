📸 How to get the best scan
  • Have the pharmacy paperwork? Scan the printed info sheet that came with your prescription — flat paper reads much better than a curved bottle.
  • Fill the frame with just the label or sheet, and hold the phone steady until the text looks sharp.
  • Good, even light. Avoid glare, and don't let the camera flash wash out the words.
  • Lay it flat / face-on. Flatten the paper, or turn a bottle so the label faces you straight on.
  • One medication or supplement at a time.
